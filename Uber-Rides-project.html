Uber Rides Dashboard

Power BI Excel
About the Project

Built entirely in Power BI, this dashboard evaluates Uber's operational performance across the full ride-booking lifecycle for 2024. Using a Kaggle dataset, the pipeline spans data cleaning in Power Query to dynamic DAX measures computing success rates, cancellations, revenue, and satisfaction metrics. The result is a six-page interactive dashboard exploring 148.77K ride records by date, vehicle type, and booking status.

Problem Statement

Uber's ride data held performance signals that traditional reporting couldn't surface. Stakeholders lacked visibility into cancellation drivers, vehicle demand, payment revenue contributions, and satisfaction variance across the fleet. Without a centralized view, decisions around driver management, customer experience, and revenue strategy lacked a reliable data foundation.

Output Interpretation

The dashboard delivers insight across six focused views, with key indicators including:

  • 148.77K total rides recorded across 2024 with a 62.01% success rate and 92,248 completed rides
  • 25.00% overall cancellation rate comprising 37,191 cancelled rides across customer and driver initiated reasons
  • Auto ranked as the most booked vehicle type at 37.1K rides, with total booking value reaching 51 Million
  • UPI dominated payment preferences at 45.01%, followed by Cash at 24.86% and Uber Wallet at 12.03%
  • Most frequent customer rating recorded is 4.90 out of 5, with 92K total ratings clustering heavily between 4.0 and 5.0 across all vehicle types
